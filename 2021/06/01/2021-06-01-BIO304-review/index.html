Instructions to candidates for final exam of BIO304

  1. Don’t write your name or ID number in this document or in the file name to enable blind marking.
  1. The examination consists of seven questions of which only FIVE questions need to be answered.
  1. Each of the five questions answered will be awarded to a maximum of 20 marks leading to a maximum of 100 marks for this exam.
  1. Type your answer directly below the appropriate question in this document and follow the instructions regarding maximum word limits.
  1. You are allowed to use the internet, but not to copy text directly; always write in your own words and refer to the source(s) you have used to come up with your answer.
  1. Make sure to regularly save your answer file onto your computer while conducting the exam.
  1. The file with your answers should be uploaded as a pdf file onto LMO before the deadline.
  1. Turnitin will be used to detect plagiarism, copying and collusion.
